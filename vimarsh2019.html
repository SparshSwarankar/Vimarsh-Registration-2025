About Vimarsh

Vimarsh is YUVA’s flagship annual conference, a forum where youth, thought leaders, and change makers come together to deliberate on India’s most pressing challenges. With a vision for the future, Vimarsh has hosted eminent speakers ranging from policymakers and academicians to social reformers and innovators.
